About me

Hi I’m Aaron , a Sicilian designer and digital artist specialized in 3D modeling, illustration, animation and video production.

I work with professional software such as Blender, Nomad, Procreate, Photoshop, Illustrator and Premiere, integrating digital techniques with 3D printing thanks to my Elegoo Neptune 3 Plus printer. My creative approach combines art and technology to give life to unique visual projects, capable of telling stories through images, objects and animations. I love experimenting with new tools, such as my DJI Mini 3 Pro drone, to capture new perspectives and bring dynamism to my works.
